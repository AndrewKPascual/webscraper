## No-Code Tools for Large Language Models (LLMs)

AI for any use-case

Harness the power of AI for any use case with Stack AI, a no-code tool that
allows you to design, test, and deploy AI workflows using models such as
ChatGPT. Our platform enables teams to create various applications, including:

Chatbots and Assistants.

    Interact with users, answer questions, and complete tasks using your internal data and APIs.

Document Processing.

    Extract insights, provide summaries, and answer questions from any document, regardless of its length.

Answer Questions on Databases.

    Connect models like ChatGPT to databases like Notion, Airtable, or Postgres to gain valuable insights about your organization.

Content Creation.

    Generate tags, summaries, and seamlessly transfer styles or formats between documents and data sources.

## Build an AI for Your Product in Minutes

Graphically connect LLMs, Vector Databases, Tools, and Data loaders.

Easily connect Large Language Models (LLMs), Vector Databases, Tools, and Data
loaders using our intuitive graphical interface. Developer teams rely on Stack
AI to automate customer support, process documents, qualify sales leads, and
search through extensive data libraries.

It's like working with Legos for AI!

![App screenshot](/tool_ss.png)

Connect Data from Any Source

    Effortlessly load data from files, websites, or any database, such as Notion, Airtable, or Postgres, into your LLM-powered application.

Tools for Your LLM

    Empower your LLM to interact with the world by seamlessly integrating with any API, including Google Search and WolframAlpha.

Templates for Any Workflow

    Jumpstart your project with a diverse library of templates for common workflows, such as chatbots and document question answering.

Experiment in Minutes

    Quickly try out multiple prompts and LLM architectures with the click of a button, allowing for rapid experimentation.

Fine-tune to Your Use Case

    Collect data and run fine-tuning jobs to create the optimal LLM that aligns with your specific product needs.

Deliver to Users in Seconds

    We host all your workflows as APIs, ensuring your users can access AI instantly, with minimal latency.
